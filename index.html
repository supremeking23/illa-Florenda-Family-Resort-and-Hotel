To keep the memories of my grandmother, my father built a resort. At first this is meant for family use only but eventually turned into a promising business. Built in 1995, Villa Florenda Family Resort is the first swimming pool resort in Cabatuan Isabela. It has 4 swimming pools for adults and kids and now hotel rooms for families to enjoy. VF also offers venue for precious life-events-baptismal, birthdays, weddings, reunions and other events.
